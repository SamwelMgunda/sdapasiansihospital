General Consultation

We provide comprehensive medical consultations for patients of all ages, offering accurate diagnosis, personalized treatment plans, and ongoing health monitoring.

Emergency and Critical Care

Our emergency department operates 24/7, staffed with skilled medical professionals ready to handle all urgent and life-threatening conditions with speed and compassion.

Maternal and Child Health Services

From prenatal care to safe delivery and pediatric checkups, we ensure the health and well-being of mothers and children through every stage of life.

Laboratory and Diagnostic Services

Our fully equipped laboratory and diagnostic unit offer precise testing, imaging, and screening services to support quick and accurate medical decisions.

Pharmacy Services

We maintain an in-house pharmacy that provides high-quality, genuine medications and professional guidance to ensure safe and effective treatment.

Surgical Services

Our hospital offers both minor and major surgical procedures, conducted in sterile, modern operating rooms by qualified surgeons and support staff.

Outpatient and Specialist Clinics

We host regular specialist clinics for conditions such as cardiology, orthopedics, ENT, dermatology, and more — ensuring every patient receives expert care tailored to their needs.

Cardiology Services

Comprehensive heart care including diagnosis, treatment, and rehabilitation of cardiovascular diseases.

Eye Care Services

Full range of ophthalmology services, from routine eye exams to advanced treatments and surgeries.

Physiotherapy Services

Personalized physical therapy programs to aid recovery, pain management, and improve mobility.

Dental Services

Comprehensive oral health services including preventive care, restorations, and orthodontics.

SDA Pasiansi Hospital

SDA Pasiansi Hospital is a referral, consultant and university teaching hospital for the Lake and Western zones of the United Republic of Tanzania. It is situated along the shores of Lake Victoria in Mwanza City. It has over 950 beds and over 1300 employees.
