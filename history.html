SDA Pasiansi Hospital

JENGO LA HOSPITALI
VYUMBA VYA UPASUAJI

SDA Pasiansi Hospital is a faith-based healthcare institution founded by the Seventh-day Adventist Church with the mission of providing high-quality medical services to all people, regardless of religious affiliation. Established to uphold the church’s long-standing values of compassion, service, and holistic healing, the hospital operates as a registered entity under the United Republic of Tanzania’s Companies Act of 2002, officially recorded in 2020 with registration number 193026.

It is fully compliant with the Ministry of Health and Social Welfare guidelines, ensuring that its operations meet national healthcare standards while preserving its faith-driven ethos. Situated in the Pasiansi area of Mwanza Region, the hospital extends its services to the broader communities of Mwanza and Simiyu regions, covering key areas around Lake Victoria where access to quality healthcare is vital.

The hospital’s history dates back to 1972, when German missionaries established a small clinic in Mwanza town to meet the community’s growing health needs. Initially located in Kirumba, the facility was later relocated to Ghana Street to improve service delivery and accommodate increasing patient numbers. Eventually, its headquarters and main operations were moved to Pasiansi, where it has since transformed into a modern, fully equipped hospital.

Over the decades, SDA Pasiansi Hospital has evolved from a modest mission clinic into a dynamic health institution, combining its missionary roots with contemporary medical standards to better serve the region’s diverse population.

Since its relocation and modernization, the hospital has experienced steady growth in both capacity and capability. This progress is driven by its ability to adapt to the rapidly changing demands of healthcare, shaped by competition, economic shifts, social needs, and technological advancements.

SDA Pasiansi Hospital prides itself on its agility — introducing new medical technologies, expanding its range of specialized services, and refining patient care protocols to match global best practices. The hospital’s expansion is not just in infrastructure but also in reputation, becoming a trusted name for dependable, compassionate, and affordable medical care in the Lake Victoria zone.

Today, SDA Pasiansi Hospital stands as a model of faith-inspired excellence in healthcare. Its team of highly skilled physicians, medical specialists, and compassionate nursing staff work collaboratively to provide personalized care across multiple disciplines. Advanced diagnostic equipment, evidence-based treatment methods, and a culture of empathy ensure that every patient receives the highest standard of care from diagnosis through recovery. Whether delivering routine check-ups, managing chronic conditions, or performing life-saving interventions, the hospital remains steadfast in its mission to safeguard the health and dignity of every individual it serves. In doing so, it carries forward the legacy of its founders while embracing innovation to meet the health challenges of today and tomorrow.

SDA Pasiansi Hospital

SDA Pasiansi Hospital is a referral, consultant and university teaching hospital for the Lake and Western zones of the United Republic of Tanzania. It is situated along the shores of Lake Victoria in Mwanza City. It has over 950 beds and over 1300 employees.
